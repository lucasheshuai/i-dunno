Recommended usage notes

For V1, I’d suggest you:

keep 3 options max whenever possible
avoid making every question “men vs women”
mix in questions about:
phenomenon
pressure
trade-offs
compatibility
trend direction
rotate categories so the feed does not feel repetitive

A strong starter set for launch would be about 15–20 of these, not all 50 at once.

Suggested launch mix for the first 18 questions

A cleaner first batch could be:

Dating: 1, 3, 6, 8
Marriage: 10, 11, 15, 16
Gender: 19, 21, 23
Money: 28, 31, 32
Values: 36, 39
Career: 44, 49

That set has good breadth and should produce visible segmentation.

One important rule for future expansion

Whenever you add new questions, check these 4 filters:

Is it understandable in under 5 seconds?
Does it create interesting group comparison?
Does it avoid sounding like a sociology paper?
Will users feel curious about the result?

If a question fails two of those, don’t ship it.
